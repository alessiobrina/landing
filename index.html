ARCHYGRAM

We use AI to recover the beauty of the lost cultural heritage

We create 3D virtual models of lost or hidden cultural heritage processing historical images from archives. The technology is based on the use of AI for the detection of monuments in images collection and the photogrammetry for 3D reconstruction.

At a glance

We use our solid academic background and many experiences in cultural heritage research to offer an high-end service at the reasonable price. Algorithms and 3D models could be used in different applications: research engine within the archival database, restoration works, AR/VR experience in museums.

Vision

We want to be the game-changer in cultural heritage, enhancing the enjoyment of virtual experiences. Archygram aims to give to everyone the chance to live among arts and architectures, even those which no longer exist or are not attainable.

Mission

This is not daydreaming. We use professional commands in AI and photogrammetry to provide a new life for lost monuments, forgotten buildings and beyond. Our know-how matches properly with a lot of collateral applications, like videogame developing, yacht design and civil architecture.
